Alien Extraction

Alien Extraction is a DIABLO-LIKE game themed in the ALIEN universe.

Players will take on the role of an elite soldier sent to an alien-infested planet to clear it of all hostile forms. The action focuses on multi-level raids, where players will have to explore, fight against saga enemies, manage their resources carefully to survive and not die in their attempt to complete the mission and discover the truth.

We completed this project in three months during our third year of university. It was our first all-class assignment, and we worked together as a team of thirty-five.

In my role as a programmer, I focused on critical tasks such as integrating scripting systems into the YMIR Engine, implementing assets and scripts for Level 2 Part 1, and ensuring quality assurance and polish. I also led efforts in bug fixing, designed the loot chest system, developed interactive dialogue and narrative elements, and enhanced user experience with quality-of-life improvements on the YMIR Engine platform. Notably, I implemented enemy health bars, crucial for enhancing gameplay dynamics.
